Asian Cuisine Vegan Cookbook

This app was created with simplicity and ease of use in mind. Users can add, view, edit and delete their own recipes.
User can also view, edit and delete recipes of other users.
With this in mind it is hoped users will personalize their cookbook, keeping the recipes they like best.



Useful Information


In the bottom left corner of the app, there is a clickable button for adding a new recipe.

The Asian Cuisine page displays three course types, click on a course to see it's recipe collection.

The My Recipes page shows a list of recipes you have added to the Cookbook. You can view, edit and delete your recipes here.

The All Recipes page displays every recipe in your collection, from newest to oldest.

The By Country page display five country categories, click on a country to see it's recipe collection.

While viewing a recipe card on any page you can choose to edit or delete it, simply click the buttons.

You can click on the Logout link when you are done.
